Tutoring

 Tutoring is an easy and fun way of learning with no travel costs and at a time that suits you.

 Online Tutorials are a great opportunity for you to receive quality bespoke feedback on your personal requirements, which can either lead to learning a good practices, inspire innovative ideas and concepts, alongside the exploration of process and techniques.

 Each space of tutoring is delivered in either 1-hour or 2-hour session. During each session, you will then be set specific tasks to learn or improve that relate to your personal needs. At the end of each session, an Independent Study outline will be discussed and drawn up for you to follow through in your own time. The whole process is very bespoke led which is ideal for your professional goals.
